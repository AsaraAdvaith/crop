Black Gram Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu

Climate: Warm and humid (25–35°C)

Soil: Fertile, well-drained loamy soil

Water Requirement: Moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Pod Borer: Flubendiamide 39.35% SC

Aphids & Whiteflies: Dimethoate 30% EC

Wilt & Root Rot: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Essential for plant establishment

Potassium (K): Helps in flowering and seed setting

Zinc Sulfate: Prevents stunted growth

Rhizobium Culture: Nitrogen fixation for better yield

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling (0–10 days): Root and leaves appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (10–40 days): Shoots and leaves develop.

3. Flowering & Pod Formation (40–70 days): Pods begin to grow.

4. Seed Development (70–100 days): Seeds mature within pods.

5. Harvesting (100–120 days): Pods dry and turn brown, ready for harvesting.
