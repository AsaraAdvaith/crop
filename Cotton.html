Cotton Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Maharashtra, Gujarat, Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka

Climate: Warm tropical (21–30°C)

Soil: Black soil (well-drained, rich in moisture retention)

Water Requirement: Moderate to high

Pesticides for Crop

Bollworm (Pink & American Bollworm): Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG

Aphids, Jassids, & Whiteflies: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL or Acetamiprid 20% SP

Cotton Leaf Curl Virus: Neem Oil + Thiamethoxam 25% WG

Root Rot & Wilt: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Encourages vegetative growth

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Strengthens root development

Muriate of Potash (K): Enhances fiber quality

Micronutrients (Zinc & Boron): Prevents flower and boll shedding

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–15 days): Seeds sprout, cotyledons appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (15–50 days): True leaves develop, roots establish.

3. Square Formation (50–80 days): Flowering buds (squares) appear.

4. Flowering & Pollination (80–100 days): Flowers bloom, pollination occurs.

5. Boll Formation & Maturity (100–150 days): Cotton bolls develop and mature.

6. Harvesting (150–180 days): Mature bolls open, cotton is picked.
