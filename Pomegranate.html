Pomegranate Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Rajasthan

Climate: Semi-arid to subtropical (25–35°C)

Soil: Well-drained sandy loam

Water Requirement: Low to moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Fruit Borer: Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG

Aphids & Whiteflies: Acetamiprid 20% SP

Bacterial Blight: Copper Oxychloride 50% WP

Root Rot: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Promotes flowering and fruiting

Single Super Phosphate (SSP): Strengthens root development

Muriate of Potash (K): Enhances fruit size & taste

Calcium Nitrate: Prevents fruit cracking

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–40 days): Seed sprouts, small plant grows.

2. Vegetative Growth (40–365+ days): Leaves, branches, and roots develop.

3. Flowering & Pollination (365+ days, during summer): Small red flowers emerge.

4. Fruit Set & Growth (30–90 days after flowering): Fruits start forming.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (180–210 days after flowering): Fruits turn red and mature.
