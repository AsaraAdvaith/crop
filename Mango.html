Mango Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Bihar

Climate: Tropical to subtropical (24–30°C)

Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter

Water Requirement: Moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Mango Hopper: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL or Thiamethoxam 25% WG

Fruit Fly: Malathion 50% EC or Lambda-Cyhalothrin 5% EC

Powdery Mildew: Sulfur 80% WP

Anthracnose (Fungal Disease): Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Enhances vegetative growth

Single Super Phosphate (SSP): Helps fruit setting

Muriate of Potash (K): Improves fruit quality

Zinc Sulfate: Prevents deficiency (dieback disease)

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–40 days): Seed sprouts, first true leaves appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (40–365+ days): Leaves, branches, and roots develop.

3. Flowering & Pollination (365+ days, during spring): Small panicles emerge.

4. Fruit Set & Development (30–90 days after flowering): Small fruits form and enlarge.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (120–150 days after flowering): Fruits mature, change color, and soften.
