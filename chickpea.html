Chickpea Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh

Climate: Cool season crop (20–25°C)

Soil: Well-drained sandy loam

Water Requirement: Low

Pesticides for Crop

Gram Pod Borer: Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG

Aphids & Whiteflies: Acetamiprid 20% SP

Wilt Disease: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Rock Phosphate (P): Helps in root growth

Urea (N) (Limited Use): Needed in early stages

Sulfur: Enhances seed production

Rhizobium Culture: Encourages nitrogen fixation

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling (0–15 days): Root system develops.

2. Vegetative Growth (15–50 days): Leaves and branches grow.

3. Flowering & Pollination (50–70 days): Flowers bloom and self-pollinate.

4. Pod Formation (70–90 days): Pods with seeds begin to form.

5. Maturity & Harvesting (90–120 days): Pods dry and are ready for harvest.
