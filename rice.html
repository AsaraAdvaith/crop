Rice Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh

Climate: Hot and humid (25–35°C)

Soil: Clayey and loamy, high water retention

Water Requirement: Very high (paddy fields)

Pesticides for Crop

Brown Planthopper: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL (systemic insecticide)

Stem Borer: Cartap Hydrochloride 4G

Leaf Folder: Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC

Blast Disease: Tricyclazole 75% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Boosts growth

Single Super Phosphate (P): Strong root development

Muriate of Potash (K): Improves yield & disease resistance

Zinc Sulfate: Prevents deficiency & stunted growth

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–20 days): Seeds sprout and grow into seedlings.

2. Tillering Stage (20–50 days): Multiple stems (tillers) develop.

3. Panicle Initiation (50–70 days): Flower clusters begin to form.

4. Flowering & Pollination (70–90 days): Grains start forming in panicles.

5. Grain Filling & Maturity (90–150 days): Grains fully develop and ripen.
