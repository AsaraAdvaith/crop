Jute Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: West Bengal, Bihar, Assam, Odisha

Climate: Hot and humid (24–37°C)

Soil: Loamy, alluvial soil (requires water-retaining capacity)

Water Requirement: High

Pesticides for Crop

Jute Stem Weevil: Chlorpyrifos 20% EC

Aphids & Thrips: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL

Powdery Mildew: Sulfur 80% WP

Stem Rot: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Boosts plant growth

Single Super Phosphate (SSP): Enhances root formation

Muriate of Potash (K): Improves fiber strength

Boron & Zinc: Prevents nutrient deficiency

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–10 days): Seeds sprout, roots emerge.

2. Vegetative Growth (10–60 days): Rapid stem and leaf growth.

3. Flowering Stage (60–90 days): Yellow flowers appear.

4. Pod & Fiber Development (90–120 days): Stems thicken, fiber matures.

5. Harvesting (120–150 days): Plants cut at the base for fiber extraction.
