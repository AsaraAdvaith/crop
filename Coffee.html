Coffee Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu

Climate: Cool and humid (15–28°C)

Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter

Water Requirement: Moderate (requires shade and rainfall)

Pesticides for Crop

Coffee Borer Beetle: Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG

Aphids & Mites: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL

Leaf Rust (Fungal Disease): Copper Oxychloride 50% WP

Root Rot & Wilt: Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Promotes leaf and branch growth

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Strengthens root system

Muriate of Potash (K): Improves bean size & flavor

Calcium & Magnesium: Enhances bean quality

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–60 days): Seeds sprout, small plants emerge.

2. Vegetative Growth (60–365+ days): Leaves, branches, and roots develop.

3. Flowering & Pollination (1–2 years): White fragrant flowers bloom.

4. Cherry Formation & Growth (3–4 months after flowering): Green coffee cherries appear.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (6–8 months after flowering): Cherries turn red and are picked.
