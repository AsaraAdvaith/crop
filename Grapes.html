Grapes Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Punjab

Climate: Mild winters and warm summers (15–35°C)

Soil: Well-drained sandy or clay loam

Water Requirement: Moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Mealybugs & Thrips: Chlorpyrifos 20% EC

Downy Mildew: Metalaxyl 8% + Mancozeb 64% WP

Powdery Mildew: Sulfur 80% WP

Anthracnose (Black Spots on Leaves): Carbendazim 50% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Boosts vine growth

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Aids in fruit formation

Potassium Sulfate (K): Enhances grape quality

Boron: Prevents poor fruit set (bunch stem necrosis)

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Vine Growth (0–40 days): Roots and vine shoots appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (40–120 days): Vine expands, new leaves and tendrils form.

3. Flowering & Pollination (120–140 days): Small flower clusters bloom.

4. Berry Set & Growth (140–160 days): Small berries appear and increase in size.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (150–180 days): Berries turn soft, change color, and sweeten.
