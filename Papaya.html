Papaya Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, Gujarat

Climate: Warm tropical (22–30°C)

Soil: Well-drained loamy soil

Water Requirement: Moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Papaya Mealybug: Thiamethoxam 25% WG

Aphids & Whiteflies: Dimethoate 30% EC

Powdery Mildew: Sulfur 80% WP

Papaya Ring Spot Virus: Neem Oil + Acetamiprid 20% SP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Enhances leaf growth

Single Super Phosphate (SSP): Strengthens root growth

Muriate of Potash (K): Improves fruit yield & quality

Magnesium Sulfate: Helps in chlorophyll production

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–30 days): Seeds sprout, roots and first leaves appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (30–120 days): Rapid leaf and stem development.

3. Flowering & Pollination (120–150 days): White/yellow flowers bloom.

4. Fruit Formation & Growth (150–210 days): Fruits appear and enlarge.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (240–270 days): Papayas change color, soften, and become ready for harvest.
