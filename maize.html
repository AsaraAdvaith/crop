Maize Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh

Climate: Warm (21–27°C)

Soil: Well-drained loamy soil

Water Requirement: Moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Fall Armyworm: Spinosad 45% SC or Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC

Stem Borer: Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG

Aphids & Jassids: Thiamethoxam 25% WG

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Enhances vegetative growth

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Supports seed formation

Potassium Sulfate: Increases resistance to drought

Boron Fertilizer: Improves cob development

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling (0–10 days): Root and shoot emerge.

2. Vegetative Growth (10–40 days): Leaves and stems grow rapidly.

3. Tasseling & Silking (40–60 days): Male (tassel) and female (silk) flowers appear.

4. Pollination & Grain Formation (60–80 days): Kernels start developing.

5. Maturity & Harvesting (80–120 days): Kernels harden and dry for harvest.
