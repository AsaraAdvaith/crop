Banana Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka

Climate: Warm and humid (27–38°C)

Soil: Fertile, well-drained, sandy loam

Water Requirement: High

Pesticides for Crop

Banana Weevil: Chlorpyrifos 20% EC

Aphids & Thrips: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL

Panama Disease (Fungal Wilt): Carbendazim 50% WP

Sigatoka Leaf Spot: Mancozeb 75% WP

Fertilizers for Crop

Urea (N): Promotes leaf growth

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Essential for root formation

Potassium Sulfate (K): Improves fruit quality

Magnesium Sulfate: Enhances chlorophyll production

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling Stage (0–40 days): Root system forms, leaves appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (40–150 days): Rapid growth of pseudostem and leaves.

3. Flowering & Bunch Formation (150–250 days): Inflorescence emerges, fruit bunches develop.

4. Fruit Development (250–300 days): Bananas start to mature.

5. Ripening & Harvesting (300–365 days): Fruits reach full size and ripen.
