Pigeon Pea Crop

About Crop

Best Regions: Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh

Climate: Warm (25–35°C)

Soil: Well-drained, deep loamy soil

Water Requirement: Low to moderate

Pesticides for Crop

Pod Borer: Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC

Wilt Disease: Carbendazim 50% WP

Aphids & Jassids: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL

Fertilizers for Crop

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP): Encourages early root growth

Potassium (K): Enhances disease resistance

Rhizobium Culture: Fixes atmospheric nitrogen

Sulfur: Essential for protein formation

Stages of Crop Growth

1. Germination & Seedling (0–15 days): First leaves appear.

2. Vegetative Growth (15–70 days): Plant height increases, branches grow.

3. Flowering (70–110 days): Yellow flowers bloom, signaling pod development.

4. Pod Formation & Seed Development (110–150 days): Seeds form inside pods.

5. Maturity & Harvesting (150–180 days): Pods dry and can be harvested.
